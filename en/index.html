Bio

A lyric soprano, Serin Park, was born in 1992 in Namyangju, South Korea. She graduated from the Sunhwa Arts High School with Wonju Choi, and completed her Bachelor’s degree in Music from the Hanyang University under the guidance of Prof. Jungwon Park. In 2021, she received her Master’s degree in Opera Singing from the Hanns Eisler Academy of Music in Berlin where she studied with Prof. Ewa Wolak.

She has won several singing competitions, as well as 3rd prize at the Ewha & Kyunghyang Music Competition and 1st prize at the Korean Franz Schubert Competition. In 2020, she received 1st prize at the Verao Classico Awards.

In 2019, she performed at the Musikforum Gendarmenmarkt, which is held once a semester in the Konzerthaus Berlin. Serin Park participated in productions: 2012 as Gilda from ›Rigoletto‹ in Seoul Art Center with conductor Heejun Choi, Violetta from ›Aufbruch‹ in Tischlerei Deutsche Oper Berlin with conductor Manuel Nawri, and Female Chorus from ›The Rape of Lucretia‹ in Kühlhaus Conductor Robin Ticciati.
